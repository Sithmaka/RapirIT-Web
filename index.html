Frequently Asked Questions

What types of devices do you repair?

We specialize in repairing desktops, laptops, and other computer peripherals. Whether it’s hardware or software, our technicians are here to help.

How long does a typical repair take?

Most repairs are completed within 1-3 business days, depending on the complexity of the issue. We strive to provide quick and efficient service.

Do you offer on-site repair services?

Yes, we offer on-site repairs for certain issues. For more complex problems, we may need to bring the device to our service center.

Can you recover my lost data?

Absolutely! We provide professional data recovery services for both personal and business data, ensuring maximum recovery rates.

How much do your repair services cost?

Our prices vary based on the type of repair. We ensure transparent pricing and provide a detailed estimate before starting any work.
